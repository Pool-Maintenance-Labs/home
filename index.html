Pool Maintenance Labs Ltd.

Who are we

Pool Maintenance Labs Ltd is a member-based not-for-profit company limited by guarantee. The objects of PML are education, research, and advocacy of liquid staking and related technologies. PML funds its objects through the receipt of grants or through agreements to supply client services to DAOs.

Our Vision

Fair and equitable and trusted world without intermediaries.

Our Mission

Our mission is high quality outcomes for DAOs and liquid staking technologies.
